Recommendations

I think everyone should read all the novels cataloged on NAD, but I understand the time constraints. So we've complied lists to help you get started. These lists are compiled from libraries, magazines, and social media sites from all around. I chose novels that appeared on all the lists (6) I consulted.The lists are not meant to be exhaustive, and your favorite novel might not be on the list. Please use the list as a starting point for choosing a great novel to read.
